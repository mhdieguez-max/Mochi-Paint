Data Deletion

Mochi Paint has no accounts, so there is no account to delete. The only data that can exist outside your device is artwork you chose to share to the community gallery (the picture, its title, and the artist name you typed).

Delete a shared artwork

Email mhdieguez@icloud.com with:

  • the artwork's title and artist name as shown in the gallery, and
  • roughly when it was shared (if you remember).

We will remove the artwork and its title/artist entry within 30 days, usually much sooner. Nothing else about you is stored, so once the artwork is gone, all of your shared data is gone.

Delete on-device data

Coloring progress lives only on your device. To erase it, clear the app's data in your phone's settings or uninstall the app.
