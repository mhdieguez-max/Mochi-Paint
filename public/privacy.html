🍡Mochi Paint

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Mochi Paint is a kawaii coloring app made for everyone, including kids. We keep things simple: no accounts, no ads, no tracking, and no personal data collection.

What stays on your device

  • Your coloring progress is saved only on your own device, so your pictures are still there when you come back.
  • Uninstalling the app (or clearing its data) removes everything.

Sharing to the community gallery (optional)

If you tap Share to gallery, the app uploads only three things to our gallery database: the picture you colored, the title you typed, and the artist name you typed (you can leave it as "Anonymous artist"). Nothing is uploaded unless you choose to share.

Please don't put real full names or personal details in the title or artist name — a nickname is perfect.

What we never collect

  • No account or sign-in, no email addresses, no passwords.
  • No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or device identifiers.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party trackers.

Deleting shared artwork

You can ask us to remove any shared artwork at any time — see the data deletion page.
